##Parts

- Arduino
- Passive Buzzer
- 5V Power Source

##Wiring

1. **5v** of **Arduino** *to* **+** of **Sensor**
1. **gnd** of **Arduino** *to* **-** of **Sensor**
1. **pin 11** of **Arduino** *to* **S** of **Sensor**

##Procedure

1. Wire Up all points.
2. Upload the Code.
